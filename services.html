What We Do

Moving & Logistics

Full-scale residential moves with trucks ranging from 15' to 26', plus trailers for smaller loads and specialty hauls. We handle local moves across the East Side and proven long-distance routes, including runs to the Detroit area and shipments to Lakewood, NJ. Our crews specialize in the items most movers won't touch: pianos, pool tables, basketball hoops, hospital beds, oversized refrigerators and freezers, and washers and dryers with full hookups. Professional packing, wrapping, and storage organization available for every job.

Home Repair & Improvement

Skilled handyman and improvement work to finish what your move starts. We hang, mud, sand, and paint drywall; tear out and install flooring; and refinish bathtubs and tubs. Appliance work includes washer/dryer and gas-range hookups and refrigerator water lines. We also handle toilet and cabinet repairs, TV mounting, mirrors, floating shelves, and curtain rods, plus outdoor jobs like gutter cleaning, siding repair, and railroad-tie retaining walls.

Specialized Skilled Labor

Seasonal Sukkah build and takedown is a cornerstone service every fall, with full crews handling installations and removals across the community. We also deliver and set up hospital beds, assemble and re-felt pool tables, build canopy beds, and provide ongoing logistics support for community Gemachs and charity events, including auction setup and event-hall deliveries.

 

Why Choose Us

Tracked-to-the-minute billing, no inflated estimates, and a roster of repeat clients across the East Side.
